How to Apply

Offline Admission Process

A clear and parent-friendly admission journey with guided steps, transparent process, and easy support from the school office.

01

Download / Collect Form

Download the admission form from the school website or collect it directly from the campus office.

02

Fill the Form Carefully

Complete all required student and parent details clearly, along with academic and contact information.

03

Attach Required Documents

Submit supporting documents such as birth certificate, photographs, previous records, and ID proof.

04

Visit the Campus Office

Submit the form at the school office and receive guidance regarding interaction, review, or admission schedule.

05

Confirmation & Enrollment

After verification and approval, complete the fee process to confirm your child’s admission.

Eligibility & Documents

Admission readiness with clear eligibility & required records

Bright Future Academy follows a simple and transparent admission process. Parents can review the eligibility guidance below and prepare the required documents in advance.

Eligibility Criteria

Basic admission requirements for smooth verification and enrollment.

Age Appropriate Admission

Student must meet the age criteria applicable to the class level at the time of admission.

Previous Academic Record

Relevant academic history or previous school record may be required for class-specific admission.

Parent / Guardian Verification

Valid identification and basic contact information of parent or guardian should be available.

Admission Interaction

For selected classes, the school may conduct a short interaction or assessment as part of admission review.

Required Documents

Please keep the following documents ready while applying for admission.
