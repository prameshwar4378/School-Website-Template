Admission Process

Simple Steps to Begin Your Learning Journey

Our admission process is designed to be clear, smooth, and parent-friendly. Follow the step-by-step path below to complete your child’s enrollment with confidence.

01

Application Form

Fill out the admission form online or visit the school office to begin the registration process.

02

Document Submission

Submit required documents such as birth certificate, previous records, photos, and ID proof.

03

Interaction / Assessment

Students and parents may be invited for a short interaction or assessment based on grade level.

04

Confirmation & Enrollment

Once approved, complete the fee process and confirm admission to secure your child’s seat.

Need help with admissions?

Contact our admission team for guidance on eligibility, documents, timelines, and fee details.
